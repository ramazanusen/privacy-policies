# Privacy Policy — MigrainePal

**Last updated:** July 3, 2026

## Overview

MigrainePal is a migraine attack tracker developed by Ramazan Usen. This privacy policy explains how the app handles your data.

## Data We Collect

MigrainePal does **not** operate any server, and does not collect, transmit, or have access to any of your personal or health data.

Everything you enter in the app — attack records, pain intensity, symptoms, triggers, medications, and notes — is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data never leaves your device unless you personally choose to share it (for example, by exporting and sending a PDF report).

## Health Data

MigrainePal is a personal tracking tool for migraine attacks and related symptoms. All health-related information you log stays on your device. We have no access to it, we do not see it, and we do not store a copy of it anywhere.

## Doctor Report (PDF Export)

The app can generate a PDF summary of your attack history. This PDF is created entirely on your device. Sharing it (via Messages, Mail, AirDrop, or any other method) is your choice, done through Apple's native share sheet. We are not involved in that transfer and never receive a copy.

## Notifications

If you enable reminders, MigrainePal schedules local notifications directly on your device using Apple's notification framework. No data is sent anywhere to schedule or deliver these reminders.

## Subscriptions

MigrainePal offers optional premium features through auto-renewable subscriptions and a one-time purchase, processed entirely by Apple through StoreKit. We do not collect or see your payment details — Apple handles all billing information according to its own privacy practices. We only receive confirmation of whether you have an active purchase, not any payment information.

## Third-Party Services

MigrainePal does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. There are no trackers of any kind in this app.

## Data Deletion

Because everything is stored locally on your device:

- You can delete individual attack records at any time within the app.
- Deleting the app from your device permanently removes all locally stored data.

Since we never receive or store your data on any server, there is no separate deletion request to make with us — removing the app accomplishes this directly.

## Children's Privacy

MigrainePal is not directed at children and is not intended for use by anyone under 17.

## Medical Disclaimer

MigrainePal is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. It is a personal tracking tool intended to help you organize information for discussions with your healthcare provider. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional about your symptoms and treatment.
